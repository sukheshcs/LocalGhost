LocalGhost is a premium network scanner and digital forensics tool designed for power users and system administrators. It provides real-time insights into your local network, helping you identify connected devices, monitor connection stability, and detect potential security vulnerabilities.

Key Capabilities:

Network Scanner: Instantly discover all devices on your Wi-Fi or LAN, including IP addresses, MAC addresses, and vendor details.
Jitter Monitor: Visualize your network latency and stability in real-time with a dynamic graph.
Security Scout: Identify open ports and potential risks on target devices.
Wake-on-LAN: Remotely wake up powered-off devices on your network.
Timeline Forensics: Track when devices join or leave your network with a detailed history log.
LocalGhost runs entirely locally on your machine, ensuring your network data remains private and secure.# LocalGhost
